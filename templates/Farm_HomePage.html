who are we ?

We are a unique online platform that provides crops from local farmers to fast-paced environmentally aware consumers in an eco friendly cheaper way, to reduce food waste and environmental damage.

Fresh

Normally, produce goes through freezing processes, to insure that it doesn’t spoil on the way. We only provide our customers with produce that is brought straight from the farm to them, which insures that it’s fresher.

Eco-Friendly

When comparing one KG of our produce to one KG of imported produce, the importing way is 5,513 times more polluting than ours. More than that, we make sure to only have compostable packaging, and minimize our ecological footprint.

Cheap

The produce in other places goes through a lot of middle men, making the end price in the supermarkets about 250% of the original price. At LocalEat, we only take what’s crucial, making the prices cheaper.
